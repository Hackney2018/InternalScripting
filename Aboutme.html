    • About Me

    Ever Since I was young I always wanted to be a Marine Biologist.

    After being diagnosed with Ashtma I knew that being a Marine Biologist Would no longer be as easy.

    I could still have been in the Marine Biology field but I would not have been able to do what I wanted in that field.

    Going under water, studying sharks and other creatures alike was always my dream.

    When we got the news I didn't even know what I wanted to do next.

    I made many changes along the way. My first choice was Video Game Development, then I was thinking about Video Game Design.

    After a while I knew Cyber Security was the field I really wanted to be in.

    Even though my mind was set on Marine Biology for a long time, I felt like Cyber Security was always something I was interested in.

    I wrestled for Whitehall my freshman year. The following years for high school I did not wrestle because I felt focusing on my academics was more important.

    I attend the Career Tech Center in Muskegon, Michigan in and have earned a couple Microsoft Certifications in Security and Networking.

    When I finish High School I plan on attending MCC for 2 years and working on CompTIA Certifications.

    After finishing 2 years of MCC, I will transfer to Ferris State University and finish my degree in Cyber Security.
